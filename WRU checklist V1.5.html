WRU CM Check List

Step 1: Power Level Measurement

1.1 Connect FieldFox N9914A and measure power level after Surge Arrestor.

1.2 Verify reading is within spec: 12 to 14 dBm.

1.3 Decision: Is power level < 12 dBm?

Step 2: VSWR Measurement (Coaxial Cable to Antenna)

Prerequisite: Ensure Surge Arrestor is removed.

2.1 Configure FieldFox N9914A for VSWR measurement.

2.2 Measure VSWR via Coaxial Cable-BPF-Splitter-Antenna.
Measured VSWR Value:

2.3 Decision: Is VSWR more than 2.0?

Step 3: Power Measurement (If VSWR > 2.0)

Perform only if VSWR in Step 2 was > 2.0.

3.1 Remove coaxial cable from either Left or Right Antenna.

3.2 Connect disconnected coaxial cable directly to N9914A (Spectrum Analyzer mode).

3.3 Measure the power level.
Measured Power Value: dBm

3.4 Decision: Is power reading within 4 to 8 dBm?

Step 4: Distance-To-Fault (DTF) Measurement

4.1 Switch FieldFox N9914A to Cable & Antenna Analyzer mode (DTF).

4.2 Ensure correct cable type and propagation velocity (VP) are configured.

4.3 Perform DTF measurement and observe the trace.

4.4 Decision: Is measurement not more than -25 dB?
